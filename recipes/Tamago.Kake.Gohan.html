Tamgao Kake Gohan (Eggs on Rice) from Silver Spoon

One of the simplest and most common breakfast dishes as shown deliciously in Silver Spoon

Its literally just egg, rice and soy sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 Bowl of Cooked Rice
  • 1 Pasteurised egg
  • Soy Sauce

Cooking Instructions

  1. Prepare a Bowl of Rice (Use a rice cooker, and wash your rice, follow the instructions for your cooker
  2. Crack an egg on the top of your rice (crack on a flat surface gently to not crack the yolk for a picture)
  3. Mix together
