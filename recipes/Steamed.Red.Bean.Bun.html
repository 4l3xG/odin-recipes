Steamed Red Bean Bun

Classic Steamed Red Bean Bun

This pastry can be found anywhere really but nothing beats creating your own

This simple pastry is made with red beans, and a flour based dough

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Red beans
  • 5 cups of Water
  • 3/4 cup of Granulated sugar
  • 1 tbsp Vanilla extract
  • 4 Cups of Flour
  • 1 tsp of Granulated sugar
  • 1 tbsp of Instant Yeast

Cooking Instructions

  1. Wash red beans then add to instant pot or pressure cooker
  2. Add 3 cups of water and cook for 1 hour on bean or chili setting
  3. Add 3/4 cup of Sugar and the vanilla extract and continue to cook while stirring, till all water evaporates
  4. Once done cooking set aside to cool
  5. Mix flour with 2 cups of lukewarm water and yeast and mix either by hand or in an electric mixer until it forms a smooth dough
  6. knead for 10 minutes
  7. Place dough in an oiled bowl covered with a damp towl or cling wrap to rise for 1 hour, should rise to twice its size
  8. Add cooled red beans into a blender and blend till a smooth paste
  9. Dust surface with flour and then knead the dough into balls slightly larger than your palms
  10. Flatten each ball and place some of the red bean paste into the middle, slowly steatch the edges of the dough and seal around the Red Bean Paste, roll gently to remove seams
  11. Leave dough filled with Red Bean paste under a wet towle for 10 minutes to rest
  12. Add partchment paper into a steamer and place the buns inside. Steam for about 12 minutes
  13. Turn off the heat for 1-2 minutes and then let the Steamed buns cool before eating
